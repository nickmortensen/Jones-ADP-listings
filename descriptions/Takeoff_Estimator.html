Job Summary:

The ideal candidate will review assigned projects to be estimated in an effort to provide an accurate and thorough takeoff of material quantities while also identifying items and tasks to be outsourced through external resources.

You will manage the sourcing processes for work and material bids to be outsourced to ensure our projects are estimated effectively and in an efficient manner to meet the time constraints that are required to handle a high bid schedule.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Gathers needed bid documents i.e. plans, specs, details and addenda as needed per assigned project.
  • Makes an initial assessment of the project and identifies areas that need additional information and/or clarification and gathers such information from the appropriate resource for processing.
  • Analyzes technical and architectural plans as required.
  • Identifies and/or sources vendors to meet scope of work and timelines.
  • Prepares RFP to be sent to vendors to obtain timely quotes and responses while addressing any questions
  • Creates, documents, and tracks RFIs to ensure scope is clear and accurate.
  • Enters and sets up bid information for Project Estimator to complete pricing more efficiently.
  • Confers directly with Project Estimator to ensure a thorough and accurate bid is submitted to sales team.
  • Writes detailed scope of work with inclusions and exclusions.
  • Supports large team of estimators.
  • Participates in customer calls as necessary.

Education, Experience or Specific Skills

  • Experience in sign industry preferred or 3 years related construction experience.
  • Three to five year of experience reading and interpreting construction plans, engineering drawings, blue prints, project scopes, and other job-site related material.
  • Strong organizational skills with ability to prioritize and manage multiple initiatives simultaneously.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and mechanical aptitude with attention to the details
  • Works on multiple projects concurrently with ability to multitask.
  • Proficient skills in Microsoft Office (Word, Excel), Adobe, Bluebeam and other related software.
  • AutoCAD, Solidworks or design software knowledge highly desirable.
  • Ability to work well in a team, as well as, independently
  • Positive “can do” attitude with a high degree of professionalism and strong work ethic.
  • Excellent verbal, written communication and presentation skills to converse intelligently with coworkers, clients, architects and subcontractors.
  • Ability to manage to timelines and due dates while adapting well to ever changing priorities.
