Job Summary:

We are looking for one (1) experienced Sign Designer to join our team either at one of our offices or in a hybrid or remote role. Sign industry experience and/or branding experience will be required.

We are looking for a highly creative sign graphic designer who can take conceptual ideas and create visual representations, both in print and electronic media.  Our ideal candidate will have expert knowledge of current design software and skilled with every step of the design process from concept to the final deliverable. Collaborating with multiple teams at our company, the graphic sign designer should be able to take direction from written or spoken ideas and convert them into a design that connects.

The successful candidate will have a thorough understanding of branding and signage, with the ability to vary style and layouts depending on the project and client’s vision. If you think you have what it takes to bring ideas to life with visual impact, we want to hear from you.

A strong background in the signage and branding industry is desired with a strong background in Adobe Illustrator and AutoCAD.

Essential Functions:

  • Work on a wide range of projects using various computer software to visualize and develop innovative sign designs that meets clients and business goals
  • Obtain input from management, ensuring designs meet organizational standards and brand expectations, express ideas accurately, and represent the company or client appropriately
  • Work independently and cooperatively on multiple projects with various internal and external teams, meeting deadlines and budget constraints, and scheduling project implementation based on workload
  • Examine existing processes, identifying flaws, and creating solutions to improve design capabilities
  • Update and maintain internal databases of designs
  • Collaborate, brainstorm, and strategize with multiple teams or clients for a wide range of materials that may include web pages, presentations, programming collateral, signage, internal communication, newsletters, and marketing materials
  • Translate strategic direction into high-quality design within an established brand identity
  • Develop concepts by hand or with software, and execute original content determining ideal usage of color, text, font style, imagery, and layout
  • Manage the design and uploading process of all project material, understanding best practices for using a content management system
  • Use trend intelligence and an understanding of the current and historical business and market to design and execute specific classifications
  • Be able to read and understand architectural drawings

Education, Experience or Specific Skills:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design or related field preferred
  • 5+ years’ relatable experience as a Graphic Designer in the sign/branding industry desired
  • Experienced in Adobe Illustrator and AutoCAD a must
  • Experience with CorelDraw also preferred
  • Demonstrable graphic design skills with a strong portfolio including technical fabrication drawings
  • Proficiency with required desktop publishing tools, including Photoshop, InDesign Quark, and Illustrator
  • A strong eye for visual composition
  • Effective time management skills and the ability to meet deadlines
  • Able to give and receive constructive criticism
  • Understanding of marketing and corporate identity
  • Knowledge of materials, fabrication methods as well as installation processes preferred
  • Experience with computer-aided design
