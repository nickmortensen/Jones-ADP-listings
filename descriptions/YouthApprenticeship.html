Position Summary:

Jones Sign, Co. is participating in the Youth Apprenticeship Program, offered through the Greater Green Bay Chamber of Commerce and Wisconsin Department of Workforce Development for the 2022-2023 school year at their De Pere, WI location!

The Youth Apprenticeship Program at Jones Sign, Co. provides High School juniors and seniors the opportunity to gain real-life experience and universal skills via work shadowing, company education classes, and career-based training programs. During this one or two-year program, Youth Apprentices will job shadow in various departments with teams of subject matter experts so they can learn where their interests and skills may lie when they join the workforce.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Maintain academic & attendance requirements to participate in the Youth Apprenticeship Program
  • Observe company rules & requirements
  • Participate in progress reviews scheduled with mentors, school personnel, and parent(s) or guardian(s)
  • Complete program/company training requirements
  • Demonstrate maturity & responsibility
  • Responsible for transportation to and from workplace

Qualifications:

  • Meet the requirements of the particular program & demonstrate competency as set forth in the program goals
  • Meet academic requirements for the program including a passing grade in courses required for admission to the Youth Apprenticeship Program
  • Attend all scheduled shadowing & training under the Youth Apprenticeship Program
