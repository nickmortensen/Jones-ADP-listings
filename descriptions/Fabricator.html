  • Use shears, grinder, torch, press brake, or drill press as needed per job specifications
  • Reading and understanding product schematics and assembly instructions

  • Ensuring that quality control standards are met
  • Adhering to assembly safety standards
  • And any other duties as assigned by the leader

    • Work Environment:

    • Physical Demands:

    • Must be able to stoop, crawl, walk, and stand for long periods at a time

    • Ability to read blueprints

    • Good organizational skills
