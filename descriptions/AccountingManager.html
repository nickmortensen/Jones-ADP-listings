Job Summary:

Essential Functions:

  • Ensures timely & accurate reporting of all financial accounting across the firm.
  • Ensures that the reported results comply with generally accepted accounting principles or financial reporting standards.
  • Provides commentary of financial results with detailed analysis
  • Leads the implementation & development of new procedures to improve department workflow
  • Provides training to new or existing staff when needed
  • Manages the accounting staff responsible for cash flow areas of billing, collections, & vendor payment
  • Presents recommendations to management on short & long-term cash flow objectives and policies
  • Works with external auditors for filing income tax returns and statutory audits
  • Ensures compliance with local, state, & federal government requirements
  • Maintains financial security by establishing internal controls
  • Makes recommendations to other departments by researching and interpreting accounting policy & applying observations & recommendations on operations
  • Performs special duties, projects, or analyses as directed by the company

Education, Experience or Specific Skills:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in accounting, finance, or related field
  • Certified Public Accountant and/or Certified Management Accountant designation preferred
  • Prior experience as a corporate accounting manager, financial manager or similar role preferred but not required.
  • Requires an understanding of current and proposed legislation and the ability to enforce accounting regulations and recommend new procedures.
  • Great communication, presentation, negotiation, and customer service skill
  • Proficiency in MS Excel and other accounting software
  • Strong analytical, interpersonal, & supervisory skill
  • Detail-oriented, self-motivated, and organized with a good understanding of financial and operational controls
  • Ability to handle multiple projects, work under pressure, meet tight deadlines and perform in a fast-paced environment
