Position Summary:

The PLC Lead is a key member of our growing company and will be responsible for assisting in the coordination of production processes through the PLC (Pack, Load, Crate) Department. Our PLC Department is responsible for the fabrication & assembly of custom shipping crates & pallets to fit our finished projects. As the work varies from day-to-day, our most successful team members have a professional work ethic, comfort adapting to change, and the ability to improvise & solve problems.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Implement a team concept into wood crate fabrication to help problem solve & improve efficiency
  • Consistently demonstrate sound safety practices, as well as the ability to lift up to 50 lbs
  • Effectively manage time to ensure on-time delivery of every project
  • Be visible & accessible on the shop floor throughout each day to field employee questions & monitor job progress
  • Comprehend work orders, shop drawings, bills of material, narratives, & clearly communicate that information to employees
  • Load trucks by hand, forklift, or crane

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Carpentry skills
  • Self-motivated & organized
