Position Summary:

We are searching for a Human Resources professional with aspirations to be part of a team that shapes the future of our company.  The Jones team looks to attract, hire, engage, develop, and retain the most talented employees in our industry, and we’re looking for a team player who can contribute to this approach.  We want a creative mind that’s looking to apply their previous accomplishments to feed our growth and enrich our culture while cultivating trust in the process. 

This is an office-based position located in our facility in De Pere, WI.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Support and guide our employees and departmental leaders with employee career pathing
  • Develop and implement a strong program to drive leadership development in support of organizational, departmental and employee needs
  • Contribute to building a strong workforce through training development and implementation
  • Utilize employee assessment tools to advance and maximize employee performance and improve deficiencies
  • Work with departmental leaders to develop compensation analysis based on employee’s performance and rankings through utilization of appropriate evaluation programs and metrics
  • Assist with developing, coordinate, and maintain an efficient onboarding experience for all new hires throughout the organization while ensuring consistency in the processes
  • Collaborate with departmental leaders to understand skills and competencies gaps within their departments
  • Establish positive employee and leadership relations to ensure all engagements are handled promptly, consistently and documented accordingly in compliance with federal, state, and local employment laws and regulations
  • Collaborate with leaders to create new or improved business initiatives, HR policies, processes, and programs
  • Ensure and facilitate, along with our Safety Leaders, that our safety and disaster plans are up to date with today’s best practices
  • Collaborate with our leaders to develop appropriate pay scales for each position and region in which we employ employees
  • Visionary outlook to support our national presence, growth and most importantly our workforce
  • Track and maintain attendance records
  • Contribute to building a culture of trust and open communication by helping applicants, employees, and leaders with employment-related inquiries and referring complex and/or sensitive matters to the appropriate team members.
  • Support and facilitates appropriate communication to maintain an informed workforce
  • Perform other duties as assigned

Required Skills/Abilities:

  • A professional approach, demeanor, and curiosity are critical to succeeding in this role
  • A bachelor’s degree in HR Management, Business Administration, or related field or equivalent experience a plus
  • 5+ years of experience in a similar role
  • PHR/SPHR or other professional related certification preferred
  • Experience supporting multi-site and multi state organization preferred
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Excellent interpersonal skills with good negotiation tactics
  • Proactive and independent with the ability to take initiative
  • Excellent time management skills with a proven ability to meet deadlines
  • Familiarity with laws, regulations, and best practices 
  • Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite or related software
