Position Summary:

As a Preconstruction Estimator you will assertively work with subcontractors and internal Jones Sign resources while compiling specifications, drawings, and proposals to bid on custom architectural projects throughout the U.S.

You will be responsible for evaluating bid specifications and drawings, ensuring all data is gathered in accordance with bid timelines to successfully submit project bids and win custom concrete, glass, and architectural metal fabrication projects. You will send requests for proposals, track and aggressively follow up with subcontractors to ensure bids are received, reviewed thoroughly, and analyzed in a timely manner to meet projects scope of work and specifications.

We would ideally like to have this position based out of our De Pere, WI location but will consider hybrid and remote candidates based on qualifications and experience levels.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Support Jones Architectural Creation’s Preconstruction and estimating efforts through the bidding, descoping, and contract execution process
  • Review project documents such as bid qualifications, drawings, renderings, and blueprints as well as specifications to identify opportunities for bidding
  • Determine timeline for scope of work based on project schedule
  • Identify and communicate any identifiable risks on project scope of work to Jones leadership
  • Complete a comprehensive take-off of the scope of work opportunity and identifying potential scope gaps
  • Identify and initiate RFI’s with prospective clients to ensure the scope of work that we are proposing is comprehensive and accurate.
  • Research and qualify appropriate vendors to meet project requirements
  • Create and administer a comprehensive request for proposal (RFP) to subcontractors and vendors
  • Facilitate and manage inbound RFI’s from suppliers and subcontractors
  • Create a formal bid tabulation sheet detailing project breakouts including quantity verifications
  • Verify and review each bidder’s proposal versus tabulation sheet to level all bids based on required scope of work
  • Present information in a concise and detailed format to Jones leadership team, business development and customers
  • Create a comprehensive inclusions and exclusions document with each proposal
  • Prepare and create detailed estimates and other deliverables
  • Complete and create project proposal for client based on bid tabulations and leveling
  • Create, negotiate, and execute a comprehensive contract with each supplier or subcontractor.
  • Manage and ensure documents are tracked accurately throughout the project
  • Develop and maintain key strategic relationships with vendors including, but not limited to, local subcontractors, suppliers, engineers, and designers
  • Create and maintain historical data and resource book for current and future projects
  • Support the transition of project from preconstruction process to the operations phase
  • Participate in proposal review and descope meetings with prospects.
  • Facilitate a project handoff to Project Management upon contract execution.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of preconstruction estimating experience
  • Coursework in business, construction, manufacturing, logistics and project management; Bachelor’s Degree preferred.
  • Experience in bidding, contract development, value engineering and negotiation
  • Ability to communicate effectively, verbally and written, with internal and external resources
  • Experienced reading and interpreting construction documents, conceptual renderings, and detailed blueprints
  • Knowledge and experience working with large subcontractors and general contractors
  • Develop relationships with both our clients and our subcontractors.
  • Prior field experience is a big plus, it gives you the knowledge and understanding needed to efficiently read a set of plans and accurately prepare each bid proposal.
  • Ability to work autonomously with a strong sense of prioritization and organizational processes
  • Proficient in MS Office Suite, Bluebeam and/or other takeoff software system
  • Strong focus on accuracy and attention to details.
  • Strong problem solving, analytical and decision-making skills.
