Position Summary:

As a Painter or Prepper you’ll have the opportunity to expand your painting and prepping skills as you work on a variety of sign and architectural projects. Our most successful Painters have a professional work ethic, comfort adapting to continual change, and the ability to improvise and solve problems. No two days are alike!

Duties & Expectations:

  • Prep items for painting, to include masking, sanding and applying bondo
  • Learn painting techniques that meet Jones Sign quality standards
  • Paint within a spray booth using HVLP guns
  • Consistently demonstrate sound safety practices, as well as the ability to lift up to 50 lbs
  • Effectively manage time to ensure on-time delivery of every project

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent

Hours: Monday-Friday, 12pm - 10pm
