Position Summary:

The Director of Construction Management is responsible for the overall success of large-scale projects.These aren't your typical store branding or rebrands of our well-known national retailers but rather this is where signage meets architectural elements associated with large construction projects.These projects are typically well known in the community and often known to the nation and for the world to see. These projects are associated with stadiums, arenas, large medical campuses, universities, museums and many more.

You are the link among all the moving parts while supporting our team of Project Managers in support of these unique projects.In this role you will ensure that the team and clients are taken care of by supporting these projects through their various phases to ensure they are delivered on time, within the assigned budget, and to client expectations.

This position can be based anywhere in the United States.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Collaborate and communicate with General Contractors, Architects, Engineers and all associated with project management
  • Creates project plan and facilitate its effective implementation along with assigned Project Managers
  • Responsible for cost management while working with estimating, purchasing, and subcontracting for proper cost containment per project and within division
  • Understands complex components of cost management including reporting, subcontractor management, and contractual scope of work
  • Oversees projects, providing expert knowledge, support, and monitoring project status to ensure on-time and on-budget completion for our clients
  • Schedules and milestone management
  • Collaborates with Safety Department and per client requirements to set site specific safety and pick plans
  • Manage and motivate project management team members by providing performance feedback, celebrating successes, and conducts quarterly reviews along with skill development and training
  • Responsible for employee hiring, development, retention, and terminations, as necessary
  • Handles situations escalated by team, ensure risks and issues are being proactively managed to minimize disruption to successful delivery
  • Oversees coordination of personnel and resources required to successfully complete project, including assigning and realigning account distribution to align with company workload and long-term goals
  • Responsible for customer retention
  • Facilitates capacity planning schedules with Operations for capacity management with both production and installation

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business, Construction, Architecture, or related field
  • Associate Degree in related field with the right mix of experience will be considered
  • Ten (10) years of sign industry knowledge with an emphasis in custom/special projects as well as high-end branding accounts
  • Ability to read, grasp and apply contractual information as a part of managing a project
  • Strong organizational, planning, problem solving and negotiation skills with the ability to resolve concerns in a positive way
  • Sound business acumen and understanding of what drives company profits, including cost accounting
  • Energized by fast-paced environment
  • Specific experience in custom projects, rebranding, construction, general contractor, field project management, installation, and industry manufacturing
  • Emphasis in project management background through general contractor projects
  • Ability to manage high dollar volume projects
