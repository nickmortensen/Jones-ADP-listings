Position Summary:

Travels to jobsites, both locally and nationally, to assemble and install finished, heavy plate and architectural metal storefront systems and glazing, at times, with little notice.

Specific Duties & Essential Functions:

  • Measure jobs and accurately relay the information
  • Layout large scale jobs
  • Overcome large jobsite issues as efficiently as possible
  • Read and interpret architectural, and/or shop, drawings.
  • Maintain and/or participate current job-related safety and training
  • Work closely with and take direction from lead foremen
  • Debrief Project Manager on completed project – noting all issues and successes.
  • Must be able to problem solve and improvise as necessary to meet plan specifications, company quality standards and deadlines.
  • Must be able to communicate and work well with general contractors, as well as project management.
  • Job requires travel approximately 3 weeks, or more, out of the month.

Qualifications:

  • Has the ability to run large equipment; Spyder crane, rough terrain forklifts etc.
  • Has the ability to understand and meet schedules
  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • CDL driver license (or ability to obtain one)
  • Ability to work out of town and stay overnight (10 days travel, 4 days home rotation)
  • Crane, construction equipment and forklift experience desired
