Position Summary:

Jones Sign seeks a Senior Quality Assistant to complete duties assigned by quality managers,  training new staff, implement quality control policies, and follow-through with implemented policies and procedures.

Reporting to the Senior Quality Manager, the Senior Quality Assistant is responsible for supporting the implementation of activities required to meet quality standards. The individual also monitors & advises on the performance of the quality management system and will produce data and generate reports on performance, measuring against set indicators. This person must also collaborate with other managers and staff throughout the organization to ensure that the Quality Assurance system is functions properly. The Senior Quality Assistant reports on changes and their implementation and provides feedback on executed training, tools, and techniques to enable others to meet our qualitystandard.

This position is an in-office position based out of our facilities in De Pere, WI.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Promotes quality achievement & performance improvement throughout the organization.
  • Participates in our progressive disciplinary process.
  • Implements quality assurance standards while reviewing existing policies and procedures.
  • Provides trend data to the site management team & support staff.
  • Defines, develops, and Deploys our product quality assurance strategy, addresses each phase of product development.
  • Trains office & production staff on quality assurance strategy.
  • Sets quality assurance compliance objectives & ensures targets are met or surpassed.
  • Supervises quality assurance team lead, quality assurance engineers and testers including the preparation and delivery of staff performance evaluations and career development activities.
  • Assesses the product specifications of the company and its suppliers, and compare them with the client's requirementsWork with the purchasing staff to establish quality requirements from external suppliers.
  • Establishes standards and clearly defined quality methods for staff
  • Sets up and maintain control and documentation procedures.
  • Identifies relevant quality-related training needs and delivers training
  • Ensures tests and procedures are properly understood, carried out, and evaluated, and product modifications are investigated, if necessary
  • Supervises technical staff in carrying out tests and checks

Qualifications:

  • Knowledge in QA and QC, should have experience leading organizations in achieving and continually maintaining quality standards such as ISO9001
  • Knowledge and experience in the manufacturing process required, preferably in a custom fabrication settings
  • Educational background such as Bachelor’s of Mechanical Engineering, Construction or related field preferred
  • At least 8 years of quality assurance / quality control experience (preferably in the manufacturing/engineering or automotive industry)
  • Ability to interact with all levels of the organization
  • Strong analytical skills and experience with implementation and administration of Quality Assurance metrics, such as defect profiles and performance to entry/exit criteria
  • Excellent problem solving, interpersonal communication and project management skills a must
  • Strong leadership skills that inspire team confidence and respect while motivating team members in a creative and effective manner
  • Excellent communication skills (speak, read & write) 
  • An orientation toward detail.
  • Added advantage: knowledge on Kaizen, Lean concepts & Six Sigma
