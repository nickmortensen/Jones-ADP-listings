Position Summary:

Billboard Installers have an opportunity to travel throughout the state on day trips to remove and/or install billboards. You'll literally be on top of the world, so comfort with heights and a strong sense of safety are a must.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Installs & maintainis all Billboard
  • Maintains tower repairs which could include tightening bolts, painting steel structures, repairing trim and various parts of the billboard sign face and structure, replacing light bulbs as needed, and assembling structures & faces when required
  • Completes all installations in a professional manner to Jones Sign safety & quality expectations
  • Uses power/manual tools: Adheres to safety regulations
  • Maintains operation vehicles; maintains construction tools, equipment, & machinery
  • Consistently demonstrates sound safety practices, as well as the ability to lift up to 50 lbs.
  • Effectively manages time to ensure on-time delivery of every project within allotted hours

Qualifications:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • A valid driver's license is required
  • Ability to complete OSHA 10-hour Construction course required
  • Preferred certifications: CDL, Crane, Signal, Rigging
  • Previous experience working at heights up to 100 ft. above ground preferre
