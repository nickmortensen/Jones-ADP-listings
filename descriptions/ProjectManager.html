Job Summary:

Jones Sign Company is looking for a Construction Project Manager to be based in the West Region of the U.S.

This individual works closely with the client providing status updates on timelines and costs as well as all client and internal documentation and reports. Project Managers are responsible for invoicing and change order documentation. You will work with all internal and external resources to coordinate and track progress of projects according to established schedules and margins.

As a Construction Project Manager, you will play a key role in the success of our client projects. Responsibilities include verifying the scope of work after the handoff from sales, nurturing the client relationship, managing the project timeline, executing on deliverables, & ensuring payment terms are met through the completion of the project. You will implement overall project plan for construction activities, delivering projects on time and within budget.

Our most successful Project Managers are tenacious with excellent critical thinking and persuasion to ensure the construction project gets done as planned while using their advanced communication skills.

Essential Functions:

  • Creates & manages project schedules, resources, & budgets
  • Communicates with the client, Jones team, subcontractors, vendors, & suppliers
  • Builds & maintains client relationships
  • Read & interpret construction drawings
  • Negotiates with vendors & subcontractors
  • Assists in documenting project phases & creates summary reports
  • Partners with field personnel to ensure proper execution onsite
  • Researches vendors for pricing, coverage, & availability
  • Creates & tracks purchase order submissions
  • Completes project close-out documents
  • Provides timely project status updates to management

Education, Experience or Specific Skills:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent education and/or experience. Construction Management degrees are preferred.
  • 5+ years of project management experience for a general contractor, retail/restaurant remodel and multi-site rollout experience is preferred.
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office and project management tracking software.
  • Demonstrated ability to interface successfully with a dedicated team of clients and employees from Project Management, Sales, Finance/Accounting, and execution resources.
