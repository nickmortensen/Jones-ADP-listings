Position Summary:

The focus of this role is to support the National Sales department via the coordination and administration of proposal related activities. A Proposal Manager must be able to work in a fast-paced environment and engage with multiple projects and tasks simultaneously. Accuracy and attention to detail are essential. Proposal Managers work very closely with various internal resources such as Sales, Project Management, Marketing, Estimating, Design and Permitting to coordinate all associated tasks to meet request for proposal deadlines.

Duties & Expectations:

  • Supports sales executives in each apect of the sales process from RFP (Request For Proposal) through contract and handoff to the Project Management team
  • Prepares responses to pre-qualification requests as well as update, organize and add information relating to all facets of the business as required to be used by all Jones staff for marketing and future pre-qualifications.
  • Meets with assigned sales executives and Directors, attend customer conference calls as required to understand the scope of work and sales strategy.
  • Reviews, organizes, and processes RFP documents within the required timelines and works with internal departments to obtain all necessary assets to accurately complete required proposal submissions on time.
  • Reviews, documents, and updates internal teams of new information for bid changes.
  • Documents and calculates final pricing sheets based on management approvals and direction.
  • Generates professional proposal documents including cover pages, form letters, bid forms, references, project profiles, etc. to create professional bid packet to be submitted per RFP guidelines.
  • Review scontacts and facilitates contract execution.
  • Manages and performs project handoffs from Sales to Project Management
  • Ensures that all company processes are correctly executed from start to finish.
  • Maintains meticulous document files, correspondence, and databases.
  • Updates company references and referrals on a regular basis.
  • Assists in the training of new sales executives.
