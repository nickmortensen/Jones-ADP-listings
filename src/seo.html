Rich Snippets/Structured Data

We've avoided using rich snippets & structured data on each web property in the jones family except jonesoutdoor.co.
All search engines scrape this basic information about a business if you bother to create it and insert it in the <head> tag. This is also known as 'structured data' and here is a link to a page telling us why structured data is important in the world of SEO. Having them on each page is basic SEO. These rich snippets should be featured in any page that has a location, a service, or a product to descibe those items. Essentially -- they describe the page. There is a template for just about anything you can think of over at the Schema.org website. Every page & service should have one of these, not to mention all the locations should have them on their individual pages. These rich snippets are a pain in the ass, but not having them in a given page is SEO malpractice. Not having them on a company's home page is a real disadvantage.
